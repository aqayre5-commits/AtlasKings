Minnesota United moved quickly to quash worrying rumors surrounding Colombian midfielder James Rodriguez on Wednesday, issuing a statement that categorically denies the player is suffering from any life-threatening muscle-wasting disease. The club stressed that Rodriguez remains in good health and continues his normal training regimen with the squad, labeling the reports as unfounded speculation that has no basis in fact.

The 32-year-old playmaker has been a key figure for Minnesota since his arrival, bringing his vast experience from Real Madrid and Bayern Munich to MLS. The club's decisive response aims to put any concerns to rest for supporters who have grown invested in Rodriguez's continued contribution to their campaign. No further details were provided regarding the origin of the rumors, but Minnesota's swift denial should reassure fans that their influential midfielder remains fit and ready to perform.
