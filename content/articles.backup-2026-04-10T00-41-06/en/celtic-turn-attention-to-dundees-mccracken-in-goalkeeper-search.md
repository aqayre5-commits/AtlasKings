Celtic's search for goalkeeping reinforcements has led them to Dens Park, where Dundee's McCracken has caught the attention of recruitment staff at Parkhead. The move suggests that Ange Postecoglou's side may be looking to strengthen between the posts, with McCracken emerging as a viable option in the Scottish top flight. The goalkeeper's performances for the struggling Dundee outfit have apparently done enough to warrant serious consideration from the Premiership leaders.

The Hoops' interest isn't limited to goalkeeping, however, as they've also been linked with a Hibernian midfielder in what could signal a broader squad refresh. Meanwhile, Motherwell manager Jens Berthel Askou has been forced to address persistent speculation about his own future at Fir Park, as the managerial merry-go-round continues to spin across Scottish football. With January's transfer window approaching, expect more movement among Scotland's elite clubs as they look to bolster their squads for the second half of the season.
