Good news for Charlton Athletic as Conor Coady has left hospital after undergoing precautionary scans following a heavy collision during the Addicks' trip to Watford on Monday. The Liverpool loanee was knocked unconscious in the incident but has since been assessed and cleared by medical staff, bringing relief to both the defender and his parent club.

While Coady's discharge is positive, his involvement in Charlton's upcoming fixtures remains uncertain as he will likely need to follow concussion protocols before being cleared to return to action. The defender was enjoying a productive spell at The Valley and will be eager to get back on the pitch once he completes the necessary medical clearances.
