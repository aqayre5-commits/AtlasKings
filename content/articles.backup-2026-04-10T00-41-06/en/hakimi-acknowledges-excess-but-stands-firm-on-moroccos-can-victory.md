Speaking to Movistar following PSG's Champions League victory over Liverpool, Hakimi reflected on a final that generated significant debate both on and off the pitch. The Paris Saint-Germain defender acknowledged that certain incidents during the match went beyond what should occur in professional football, showing maturity in recognizing the intensity that sometimes boils over in high-stakes continental competitions. However, Hakimi remained resolute in his conviction that Morocco's triumph was deserved and legitimate.

The Lions of the Atlas' captain emphasized that despite the controversial moments, Morocco's quality and determination ultimately prevailed against a strong Senegalese side. For Moroccan football fans, Hakimi's balanced approach—accepting that excesses occurred while firmly defending the team's achievement—reflects the reality that Africa's most prestigious tournament often brings out raw passion. The victory stands as a testament to Morocco's football development, regardless of the drama that surrounded it.
