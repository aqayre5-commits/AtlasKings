In a defiant gesture that underscores the ongoing drama surrounding this year's Afcon tournament, Senegal's players proudly displayed the continental trophy before Saturday's encounter with Peru at the Parc des Princes. The parade, attended by jubilant supporters, served as an emotional show of solidarity following CAF's shock decision to strip the Lions of Senegal of their hard-earned title—a decision that has sent shockwaves through African football and ignited fierce debate about governance and fairness in the sport.

The circumstances remain deeply troubling for Senegalese football. What should have been an unblemished triumph has been tainted by administrative intervention, leaving fans questioning the legitimacy of the punishment and its precedent for future competitions. The friendly against Peru offers the squad a chance to refocus on the pitch while the controversy simmers off it, though the emotional toll of the title removal is unlikely to dissipate anytime soon.
