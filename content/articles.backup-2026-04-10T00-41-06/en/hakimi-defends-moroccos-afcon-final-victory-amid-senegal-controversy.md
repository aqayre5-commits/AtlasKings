Achraf Hakimi has broken his silence on the controversial 2025 AFCON final, insisting that Morocco's victory was deserved despite the heated scenes that overshadowed the match in Rabat. Speaking to Movistar following PSG's impressive 2-0 Champions League victory over Liverpool, the Moroccan defender acknowledged the tension-filled nature of the encounter but stood firm in his conviction that his national team's performance warranted the continental crown. "It was a difficult and tense match," Hakimi said, though he stopped short of elaborating on the specific incidents that marred the encounter.

The PSG defender's measured response reflects the broader sentiment among Moroccan fans and officials, who view the final result as a just conclusion to a strong tournament campaign. While Hakimi admitted he was not entirely satisfied with how events unfolded, his focus remained on Morocco's collective achievement in reclaiming African football's biggest prize. The comments come as the football world continues to dissect the controversial finish, with many supporters eager to move forward and celebrate Morocco's success rather than dwell on the controversial moments.
