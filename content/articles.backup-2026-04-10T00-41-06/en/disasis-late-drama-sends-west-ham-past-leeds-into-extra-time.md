In a match that seemed destined for a Yorkshire heartbreak, West Ham found salvation in the final moments when Disasi pounced on a loose ball in the Leeds box to level the score and force extra time. The defender's poaching instinct proved crucial as West Ham avoided a stunning upset that would have sent Leeds through to the semi-finals. The goal sent the away support into raptures and deflated the Elland Road crowd just when Leeds appeared to have sealed a memorable upset.

The equaliser epitomised West Ham's refusal to surrender despite a dominant Leeds performance. The hosts had controlled large periods of the match and appeared to be heading for a giant-killing result, but Disasi's timely intervention denied them a place in the final four. With the quarter-final now heading to extra time, both teams face a gruelling battle to secure their passage to Wembley, though the momentum has dramatically shifted in West Ham's favour after their last-gasp reprieve.
