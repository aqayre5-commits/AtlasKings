In a dramatic turn of events that has sent shockwaves through African football, CAF has officially reversed the result of the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations final, stripping Senegal of their victory and awarding the continental crown to Morocco instead. The decision comes following a formal review of circumstances surrounding the match, marking one of the most significant administrative interventions in recent AFCON history. Morocco's players and supporters can now rightfully celebrate what many will view as justice restored on the continent's biggest stage.

The reversal represents vindication for the Atlas Lions, who had contested the legitimacy of Senegal's win. While details of CAF's specific findings remain subject to scrutiny, the decision underscores growing concerns about match integrity and administrative standards at the continental level. For Moroccan football fans, this outcome caps what has been an extraordinary campaign, cementing the national team's status as worthy champions of Africa.
