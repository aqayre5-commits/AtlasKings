Celtic showed their mettle at Dundee, grinding out a crucial win that demonstrates the fighting spirit O'Neill has instilled since taking charge. The interim manager praised his players for their professionalism in what was clearly a demanding fixture, emphasizing that championships are won through performances like this rather than stylish displays. "This is what matters in the run-in," O'Neill reflected, highlighting how his team refused to be complacent despite their status as favorites.

The victory keeps Celtic's title ambitions alive and sends a clear message to their rivals about their determination to challenge for the crown. O'Neill's ability to maintain squad morale and cohesion during a transitional period has been crucial, with players responding to his leadership on the pitch. As the Premiership race intensifies, Celtic's proven mental toughness could prove decisive in the months ahead.
