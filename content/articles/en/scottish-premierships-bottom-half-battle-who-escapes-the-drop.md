Consequently, survival often hinges on stability—maintaining a functioning first XI and building genuine cohesion across a campaign. Teams that have managed this have often found their way clear of danger; those that have not have drifted toward the abyss. Head-to-head records between relegation contenders become crucial; a positive result against a direct rival can provide both points and psychological advantage.
